Module 01

Daily Money Rules

Three non-negotiable rules applied every single day. No app. No spreadsheet. No complexity. Just structure that rewires your defaults over time.

01
⏱

24-Hour Delay Rule

Before any non-essential purchase, wait 24 hours without exception. If you still want it tomorrow with the same conviction, revisit. Most desires dissolve by morning — and that gap is pure savings.

02
🚫

No Spend Days

Specific days each week where zero money leaves your account. Not a reduction — a full stop. This trains restraint as reflex rather than conscious effort. The muscle grows strongest under total constraint.

03
⚖

Impulse Filter Checklist

Every unplanned purchase passes three gates: Is this budgeted? Does it serve an active goal? Can I wait 24 hours? All three must clear. One failure and you don't proceed. The friction is the feature.

Module 03

Spend Awareness
Framework

Every purchase evaluated against three categories. No grey zones. Radical clarity replaces comfortable ambiguity.

Need

Non-negotiable expenses sustaining your life and earning capacity. These are protected — targets for optimization, never elimination.

"Would removing this directly harm my health, shelter, or ability to generate income?"

Want

Quality-of-life purchases that are valid — when planned. The rule: Wants are decided in advance, never in the moment of temptation.

"Did I make this decision before the emotion, the store, or the app appeared?"

Waste

Purchases serving neither genuine need nor planned want. The impulse buys and subscriptions you regret before the confirmation email arrives.

"Would I make this exact choice again tomorrow with full clarity and zero pressure?"

Module 04

30-Day Challenge

Gamified. Progressive. Engineered to rewire financial behavior — not through willpower, but through architecture that makes the right choice the easiest choice.

7Days 01–07

Phase I — Awareness

You cannot fix what you cannot see. Week one is radical visibility with zero judgment. Track everything. Label everything. This week is data collection, not correction.

  • Log every purchase same-day without exception
  • Identify your top 3 spending categories by volume
  • Calculate your exact weekly outflow
  • Apply Need / Want / Waste labels to all transactions
7Days 08–14

Phase II — Control

Now you act. Daily rules enforced without exception. No-spend days mandatory. The 24-hour delay becomes reflex. Behavior change begins here — not with motivation, but with structure.

  • Execute minimum 3 full No-Spend Days
  • Apply 24H rule to every unplanned purchase
  • Eliminate the single largest Waste category
  • Complete your first full Weekly Reset
16Days 15–30

Phase III — Optimization

The system is operational. Now refine every variable. By Day 30, financial discipline is no longer a practice — it's your identity.

  • Redirect all Waste recovery to savings or debt elimination
  • Conduct full fixed-cost optimization review
  • Complete two additional Weekly Resets
  • Define your concrete Month 2 financial target
