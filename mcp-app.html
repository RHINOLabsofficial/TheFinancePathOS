Module 01 · Tool

Impulse Filter

Before you buy anything unplanned, run it through the three gates. All three must clear.

Purchase Under Review

Gate 1 — Is this purchase budgeted or planned for this week?

Gate 2 — Does this purchase directly serve an active financial or life goal?

Gate 3 — Can you wait 24 hours and revisit this decision tomorrow?

Filter Logic
All Three Pass
The purchase is pre-approved. Proceed consciously.
One or Two Pass
Apply the 24-hour rule. Come back tomorrow with fresh eyes.
None Pass
Do not proceed. This is exactly the purchase the filter was built to stop.
